# Visa Process: Skills in Demand (Subclass 482)

*   **Visa Type:** Skills in Demand Visa (Subclass 482) - *Note: This visa replaced the Temporary Skill Shortage (TSS) visa on December 7, 2024.*
*   **Nationality:** Mexican
*   **Visa Stream:** Specialist Skills Stream (due to salary > AUD $141,210)
*   **Sponsoring Company:** Bundle of Rays (BoR)
*   **Role:** CTO
*   **Salary:** AUD 175,000 - 200,000 per year
*   **Start Date:** February 2026
*   **English Language Test (Hector):** Required. Chosen test: IELTS. Minimum scores apply from September 13, 2025 (IELTS 5.0 in each band).
*   **English Language Test (Liz):** Required for 'Functional English' as she studied in Spanish.
*   **English Language for Dependents:** No English language requirement for dependent children.
*   **Skills Assessment:** Highly likely to be required for CTO role by Australian Computer Society (ACS).
*   **Application Status:** Pending
*   **Timeline:**
    *   Specialist Skills Stream: 90% processed within 67 days (target ~7 days); most decisions within 4-7 weeks, nearly all by ~10 weeks.
*   **Costs (Estimated):**
    *   **Applicant Costs (for family of 4):**
        *   Visa Application Charge: AUD $3,210 (main applicant) + AUD $3,210 (wife) + AUD $805 (Natalia) + AUD $805 (Alex) = **AUD $8,030**
        *   Health Examinations: Approx. AUD $1,400 - $2,000 (for 4 people)
        *   Police Checks: Approx. AUD $100 - $400 (for 2 adults)
        *   English Test (IELTS x2): Approx. AUD $820 - $900
        *   Skills Assessment (if required for CTO): Varies
        *   Translation/Certification: Approx. AUD $25 - $100 per document
    *   **Employer Costs (Bundle of Rays):**
        *   Standard Business Sponsorship (SBS) Application Fee: AUD $420
        *   Nomination Fee: AUD $330
        *   Skilling Australians Fund (SAF) Levy: AUD $1,800 per year of visa (e.g., AUD $7,200 for a 4-year visa, assuming BoR turnover > $10M)
