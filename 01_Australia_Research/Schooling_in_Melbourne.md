## General Enrollment Process for International Students
*   **Application through IED:** Apply via the Department of Education's International Education Division (IED).
*   **Process:** Review terms, submit application (often via agent), receive/return offer, pay invoice, receive Confirmation of Enrolment, apply for visa.
*   **Requirements:** Appropriate visa, meet age eligibility, demonstrate English proficiency (for secondary), have Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC).
*   **Fees (2025):**
    *   Junior Secondary (7–10): AUD $18,488 annually.
    *   Senior Secondary (11–12): AUD $20,748 annually.
    *   VCASS (Senior): AUD $28,161 annually.
    *   Additional non-tuition fees apply (application, support, etc.).
    *   *Note:* Fees do not cover uniforms, books, excursions. 482 visa dependents likely pay these fees.
