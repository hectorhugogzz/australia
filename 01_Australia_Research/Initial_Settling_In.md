# Initial Settling-in Period in Melbourne

## Key Tasks for First Few Weeks
1.  **Temporary Accommodation:** Secure short-term housing before you arrive (e.g., Airbnb, serviced apartment).
2.  **Activate Bank Account:** Visit your chosen bank branch to verify your ID and get your accounts fully operational.
3.  **Get a Local Phone Number:** Purchase Australian SIM cards.
4.  **Apply for a Tax File Number (TFN):** Apply online via the ATO website as soon as you arrive. This is crucial for employment.
5.  **Get Myki Cards:** For using Melbourne's public transport.
6.  **Health Insurance:** Register for Medicare (if eligible) or ensure your private health insurance is active.
7.  **Find Long-Term Housing:** Start attending rental inspections.
8.  **School Enrollment:** Formally enroll Alex in his chosen school.
9.  **Driver's License:** Begin the process of converting your Mexican licenses to Victorian ones.

## Realistic Initial Budget
It is wise to have an emergency fund covering at least three months of living expenses.

*   **Initial Upfront Expenses:**
    *   **Temporary Accommodation (2-4 weeks):** Budget AUD $2,000 - $6,000+.
    *   **Rental Bond (for long-term lease):** Typically 4-6 weeks' rent (e.g., AUD $3,200 - $4,800 for an $800/week rental).
    *   **Rent in Advance:** Typically 2-4 weeks' rent (e.g., AUD $1,600 - $3,200).
    *   **Utility Connection Fees:** Budget AUD $500 - $1,000.
    *   **Initial Furnishing:** If buying new/used, this will be a significant upfront cost (see `Moving_and_Furnishing.md`).

*   **Estimated Monthly Living Costs (After Initial Setup, for a family of four):**
    *   **Total (including rent):** Around **AUD $7,000 - $9,000+** per month.
    *   **Breakdown (Excluding Rent):** Approx. **AUD $5,756.50** per month.
        *   **Utilities:** AUD $150 - $250
        *   **Water:** AUD $50 - $100
        *   **Internet & Phone:** AUD $70 - $120
        *   **Groceries:** AUD $1,000 - $1,500
        *   **Transportation:** AUD $150 - $300+ (depending on car usage)
        *   **Leisure:** AUD $200 - $400+
