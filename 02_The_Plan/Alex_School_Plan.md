# Alex's Schooling Plan (2027)

Alex will attend **Port Melbourne Secondary College** from the start of the 2027 school year. This document outlines the enrolment plan and key considerations.

---

### **Decision: Port Melbourne Secondary College**

*   **School:** Port Melbourne Secondary College (public, co-educational).
*   **Year Level:** Enters **Year 8 in 2027** (to confirm with the school based on age and prior schooling).
*   **Why:** Strong STEAM focus (robotics, fabrication lab), aligned with Alex's interest in tech, software and engineering. Bayside location keeps the family in one suburb with Natalia at nearby Albert Park College.
*   **Fees:** **Exempt from standard tuition** as a 482 visa dependent. Budget ~$1,000–$2,000/year for materials, uniforms and contributions.
*   **Taekwondo:** Joon No's Taekwondo and Black Dragon Taekwondo are nearby options to continue his training (Red Belt).

---

### **Important: Two Different Catchment Zones**

Port Melbourne Secondary College and **Albert Park College** (Natalia's school) have **separate catchment zones**. A single rental address may not fall within both.

*   **[ ] Action:** Before signing a lease, **confirm with both schools** whether the chosen address is in-zone (or accepted) for each. Prioritise an address in the **Port Melbourne / Fishermans Bend area** that also allows Natalia's enrolment at Albert Park College, or discuss out-of-zone enrolment options with the schools.

1.  **[ ] Secure In-Zone Housing (Dec 2026 – Jan 2027):**

2.  **[ ] Contact Port Melbourne Secondary College (Nov–Dec 2026):**

3.  **[ ] Submit Application via Study Victoria (if required):**

4.  **[ ] Prepare Documents (digital):**
