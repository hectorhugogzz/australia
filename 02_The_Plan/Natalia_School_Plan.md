# Natalia's Schooling Plan (Year 11, 2027)

This document outlines the plan for Natalia's schooling in Melbourne from the start of the 2027 school year. The strategy has shifted from a full-time private arts school to **mainstream public schooling at Albert Park College**, combined with **Stage School Australia** for specialist performing-arts training as an extracurricular.

---

### **The Strategy: Public School + Specialist Extracurricular**

*   **Day School:** **Albert Park College** (public, co-educational, Years 7–12). Natalia enters **Year 11 in 2027**.
    *   **Why:** As a 482 visa dependent, Natalia is **exempt from standard tuition fees** in Victorian government schools. Albert Park College is a strong, well-regarded bayside school with an established arts culture, and keeps Alex and Natalia in the same suburb.
    *   **Cost:** ~$1,000–$2,000/year for materials, uniforms, subject levies and voluntary contributions (no tuition).
*   **Performing Arts:** **Stage School Australia** — [https://stageschool.com.au/](https://stageschool.com.au/)
    *   **Format:** After-school / weekend classes in Musical Theatre, Acting, Singing and Dance. This gives Natalia elite triple-threat training without sacrificing a mainstream academic path.
    *   **Entry:** Audition / application (see below).

---

### **Action Plan**

#### **Phase 1: Preparation (from now – Dec 2026)**
*   **[ ] Skills:** Continue local musical theatre / dance / singing classes in Puebla to keep triple-threat skills sharp for the Stage School audition.
*   **[ ] Audition Material:** Prepare a contrasting monologue, a musical-theatre song (16–32 bars) and a short dance routine, in case Stage School requires an audition or placement.
*   **[ ] Documentation:** Gather recent school reports from Colegio Humboldt (translated to English) for the Albert Park College enrolment.

#### **Phase 2: School Enrolment (Nov 2026 – Jan 2027)**
*   **[ ] Confirm Zoning:** Verify that the chosen rental address falls within the **Albert Park College** catchment zone (their zone differs from Port Melbourne Secondary College's — see [Alex_School_Plan.md](./Alex_School_Plan.md)).

*   **[ ] Documents ready (digital):** Passport & Visa Grant Notice, translated birth certificate, translated Colegio Humboldt reports, proof of Melbourne residential address (lease).

#### **Phase 3: Stage School Australia (from arrival, Feb 2027)**
*   **[ ] Enquire & Audition:** Contact Stage School Australia on arrival (or before, by email) to confirm the intake for Term 1 2027, class levels, audition/placement requirements and fees.
*   **[ ] Enrol:** Enrol Natalia in the appropriate Musical Theatre / triple-threat stream as an after-school activity.

---

### **Key Risk & Recommendation: Term 1 Start**

The 2027 Victorian school year (Term 1) begins in **late January / early February 2027** — the same window as the family's arrival. To avoid Natalia missing the start of Year 11:
*   **Recommended:** Secure the long-term rental (and therefore the in-zone address) **before arrival** via remote application, then lodge the Albert Park College enrolment in Nov–Dec 2026.
*   **Alternative:** Arrive in **mid-January** to secure housing and complete enrolment before Term 1 begins.
