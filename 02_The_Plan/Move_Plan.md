*   **[ ] Furnishing:** Order office/gaming equipment:

    *   Alex's Room: gaming desk and chair.
*   **[ ] Health:** Confirm Overseas Visitor Health Cover (OVHC) is active; register for Medicare if eligible.
*   **[ ] Tax — Medicare Levy Exemption:** As a 482 holder from a non-reciprocal country (Mexico), Hector is not eligible for Medicare and can claim the **2% Medicare Levy Exemption** (worth ~$5,000/yr on the $250k salary). Apply to Services Australia for a **Medicare Entitlement Statement (MES)** covering the financial year, and claim the exemption in the tax return. Renew the MES each financial year.
