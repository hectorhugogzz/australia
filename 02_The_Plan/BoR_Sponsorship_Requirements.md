# Bundle of Rays (BoR) Sponsorship Requirements & Timeline
## Subclass 482 (Skills in Demand) Visa - CTO Role

This document outlines the specific tasks, requirements, costs, and timeline that Bundle of Rays (BoR) must follow to sponsor Hector Gonzalez for the Subclass 482 visa.

### Phase 1: Standard Business Sponsorship (SBS)
*If BoR is already an approved sponsor, this step is not needed.*
*   **Official Link:** [Become a Sponsor (Home Affairs)](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/employing-and-sponsoring-someone/sponsoring-workers/becoming-a-sponsor)
*   **Task:** Apply to the Department of Home Affairs to become an approved Standard Business Sponsor.
*   **Requirement:** Prove BoR is a legally established and operating business in Australia.
*   **Timeline:** **Kick-off Jul 27, 2026.** Can be lodged at the same time as the Nomination. Note: LMT advertising (below) should start immediately so the 4-week clock runs in parallel.
*   **Cost:** AUD $420

### Phase 2: Nomination
*This is the critical step that must be lodged before the visa application.*
*   **Official Link:** [Nominate a Position (Home Affairs)](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/employing-and-sponsoring-someone/sponsoring-workers/nominating-a-position)
*   **Task:** Formally nominate Hector for the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) position.
*   **Key Requirements:**
    1.  **Genuine Position:** Prove the CTO role is a genuine and necessary position within BoR.
    2.  **Salary Requirements:** Ensure the offered salary (**AUD $250,000 + super**) meets the Annual Market Salary Rate (AMSR) and is above the Temporary Skilled Migration Income Threshold (TSMIT) — comfortably clears both.
    3.  **Labour Market Testing (LMT):**
        *   **CRITICAL:** Unless an exemption applies (e.g., international trade obligations), BoR *must* have advertised the CTO position on at least three prominent platforms (including [Workforce Australia](https://www.workforceaustralia.gov.au/)) for a minimum of **4 weeks** within the 4 months prior to lodging the nomination. Start this advertising at kick-off (Jul 27, 2026) so it is complete before the nomination.
*   **Timeline:** **Lodge the Nomination Sep–Oct 2026** (once funding lands and LMT is complete), then provide the TRN to Hector. The visa application cannot be approved until the nomination is approved.
*   **Costs:**
    *   **Nomination Fee:** AUD $330
    *   **Skilling Australians Fund (SAF) Levy:** ~AUD $7,200 (For a 4-year visa, calculated based on business turnover).

### Phase 3: Provide TRN to Applicant
*   **Portal for Lodgement:** [ImmiAccount Login](https://online.homeaffairs.gov.au/lusc/login)
*   **Task:** Once BoR lodges the Nomination (Phase 2), they will receive a **Transaction Reference Number (TRN)**.
*   **Requirement:** BoR must provide this TRN to Hector. Hector needs this number to link his family's visa application to BoR's sponsorship.
