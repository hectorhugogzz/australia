# Visa Plan Summary: Skills in Demand (Subclass 482)

**Goal:** Secure the 482 work visa to support a **remote work start** with **Bundle of Rays (BoR)** in **Oct/Nov 2026** and a physical move to **Melbourne** in **late January / early February 2027**, in time for the children to begin the 2027 school year (Alex → Port Melbourne Secondary College; Natalia → Albert Park College + Stage School Australia).

---

## 1. The Visa

*   **Visa Subclass:** **Skills in Demand Visa (Subclass 482)**
*   **Stream:** **Specialist Skills Stream**
    *   *Why:* This stream is for applicants with a guaranteed annual income of at least **AUD 135,000** (your offer is **AUD 250,000 + super**, comfortably above the threshold). It is distinct from the "Core Skills" stream and generally has faster processing times.
*   **Key Feature:** Expedited processing target of **7 days** for the visa application itself.
*   **Official Link:** [Department of Home Affairs - Skills in Demand Visa](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/skills-in-demand)
    *   *Note: This visa replaced the Temporary Skill Shortage (TSS) visa in late 2024.*

## 2. Sponsor Responsibilities (Bundle of Rays)

**Objective:** BoR must complete the sponsorship and nomination stages *before* you can lodge your personal visa application.

| Action | Description | Timeline Deadline |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Standard Business Sponsorship (SBS)** | Apply to become an approved sponsor (if not already). Valid for 5 years. | **Begins Jul 27, 2026** |
| **2. Labour Market Testing (LMT)** | Advertise the CTO role for 4 weeks (unless exempt). | **Aug 2026** |
| **3. Nomination** | Nominate the **Chief Technology Officer (CTO)** position (after funding lands). | **Sep–Oct 2026** |
| **Requirements** | • Prove the position is genuine.<br>• Confirm salary meets the Specialist Skills threshold.<br>• Pay the Skilling Australians Fund (SAF) levy. | |

**Critical Handshake:** BoR must provide you with the **Transaction Reference Number (TRN)** from their approved nomination so you can link your visa application to it.

## 4. Strategic Alignment & Goals

This timeline is designed to de-risk your three major milestones:

### **Milestone A: Remote Work for BoR (Oct/Nov 2026)**
*   **Plan:** Hector begins working **remotely from Mexico** for BoR in Oct/Nov 2026 — this does not require the visa (he is still onshore in Mexico), so income starts building before the move.
*   **Benefit:** Several months of BoR salary accrue before departure, strengthening the settlement fund.

### **Milestone B: Visa Grant (Nov–Dec 2026)**
*   **Plan:** Lodging the visa on TRN receipt (Oct/Nov) under the **Specialist Skills Stream** targets a grant within ~4–10 weeks.
*   **Benefit:** Grant by year-end provides legal certainty and the Visa Grant Notice needed for the children's school enrolments.

### **Milestone C: Move to Australia (Late Jan / Early Feb 2027)**
*   **Plan:** With the visa granted in late 2026, the family moves in time for the **2027 school year (Term 1)**, with a buffer to finalize flights, temporary housing and an in-zone rental.
