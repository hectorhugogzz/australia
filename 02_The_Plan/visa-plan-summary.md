# Visa Plan Summary: Skills in Demand (Subclass 482)

**Goal:** Secure the necessary work visa to allow for a remote work start with **Bundle of Rays (BoR)** in **March/April 2026** and a physical move to **Melbourne** in **July 2026**. This timeline also supports **Natalia's VCASS application** (deadline approx. April 2026).

---

## 1. The Visa

*   **Visa Subclass:** **Skills in Demand Visa (Subclass 482)**
*   **Stream:** **Specialist Skills Stream**
    *   *Why:* This stream is for applicants with a guaranteed annual income of at least **AUD 135,000** (your offer is AUD 175k-200k). It is distinct from the "Core Skills" stream and generally has faster processing times.
*   **Key Feature:** Expedited processing target of **7 days** for the visa application itself.
*   **Official Link:** [Department of Home Affairs - Skills in Demand Visa](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/skills-in-demand)
    *   *Note: This visa replaced the Temporary Skill Shortage (TSS) visa in late 2024.*

## 2. Sponsor Responsibilities (Bundle of Rays)

**Objective:** BoR must complete the sponsorship and nomination stages *before* you can lodge your personal visa application.

| Action | Description | Timeline Deadline |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Standard Business Sponsorship (SBS)** | Apply to become an approved sponsor (if not already). Valid for 5 years. | **January 31, 2026** |
| **2. Nomination** | Nominate the **Chief Technology Officer (CTO)** position. | **February 15, 2026** |
| **Requirements** | • Prove the position is genuine.<br>• Confirm salary meets the Specialist Skills threshold.<br>• Pay the Skilling Australians Fund (SAF) levy. | |

**Critical Handshake:** BoR must provide you with the **Transaction Reference Number (TRN)** from their approved nomination so you can link your visa application to it.

## 4. Strategic Alignment & Goals

This timeline is designed to de-risk your three major milestones:

### **Milestone A: Remote Work for BoR (March/April 2026)**
*   **Plan:** By lodging the visa on **March 15th** under the **Specialist Skills Stream**, we target a grant date between **March 22nd - March 31st** (approx. 7 business days).
*   **Benefit:** Having the visa granted by late March provides legal certainty for BoR to onboard you as an Australian employee for a potential April 1st start.

### **Milestone B: Natalia's VCASS Application (Before April 2026)**
*   **Plan:** The VCASS application window closes in **April**.
*   **Benefit:** Lodging the visa by March 15th allows her to include the **Visa Transaction Reference Number (TRN)** on her school application, proving the relocation is officially in progress.

### **Milestone C: Move to Australia (July 2026)**
*   **Plan:** With the visa granted in March, you have a comfortable 3-month buffer to finalize logistics (flights, shipping, temporary housing) without the stress of pending immigration status.
